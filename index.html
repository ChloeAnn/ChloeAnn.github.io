Who Am I

Ethical AI is possible and necessary and I intend to see that it materialises.

Hi I'm Chloe.
I was born and raised in Newport, South Wales.
Ydw, dwi'n siarad Cymraeg ychydig ac rydw i'n ymarfer yn gyson.

I am passionate about mathematics and more importantly demystifying mathematics for young people. I have collaborated with some amazing organisations that work tirelessly to to encourage STEM learning in the black community.

My love for mathematics and modelling the world around us led me to speech technologies 7 years ago. My PhD focused on Speech Recognition, my first three years in industry I worked on Speech Synthesis systems and I have now found myself at home working on Knowledge Graphs and NLP techniques.

I want nothing more than a quiet, peaceful life as a researcher, however this isn't an option for me while the research community is exclusive, therefore I advocate for a more diverse, gender balanced community and fiercly believe that educational diversity is the key to innovation.
